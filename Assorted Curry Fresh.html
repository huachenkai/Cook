Assorted Curry Fresh

  • Dish's Materials

    • Carrot 100g

    • Egg two

    • The right amount of Curry sauce

    • Wax gourd 70g

    • Cowpeas 100g

    • Potato 100g

    • Okra 80g

    • The right amount of the red pepper

    • The right amount of salad oil

  • Dish's Information

    • Difficulty: Normal

    • Time: One hour

    • Taste: Hot and Sour

    • Technology: Burn

First Step:

Prepare all the ingredients, the egg omelet, the carrot slices, the potato cut, the cowpea cut, the okra cut, the winter melon slice.

Second Step:

Stir-fry the beans with a moderate amount of water, add carrots and potatoes, stir well, add the okra and squash, and toss and mix well.

Third Step:

Red chilli is broken and added, and you can add some spicy food,add the curry sauce to the water.

Forth Step:

This is usually two tablespoons plus a small spoon, and then the juice of half a lime.

Fifth Step:

Mix the sauce with the sauce, and start to peel the dish and add the tofu.

Sixth Step:

And we'll stew it until it's thick.
