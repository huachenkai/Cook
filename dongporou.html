Dongpo Pork

  • Dish's Materials

    • Pork 1000g

    • Rock candy 60g

    • Dry red dates 5g

    • ginger 5g

    • cold water 75g

    • Longan 450g

    • Sugar 50g

    • rum 20ml

    • Ground cinnamon A quarter of small spoon

    • Bread crumbs 40g

  • Dish's Information

    • Difficulty: Normal

    • Time: A few hours

    • Taste: Sour and Sweet

    • Technology: Baking

First Step:

Cut into blocks of mahjong.

Second Step:

The bottom of the casserole covered with a layer of broken bamboo.

Third Step:

Put on the whole tree cut into large shallot and ginger.

Forth Step:

Add wine, soy sauce and sugar, the fire, after the fire to boil fire, cover and simmer for 3 hours.

Fifth Step:

The middle 1.5 hours or so, open the lid over, make the skin up, continue to simmer with.

Sixth Step:

Stew meat carefully into the heat resistant containers, steam for 30 minutes.
