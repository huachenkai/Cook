India Curry Wings

  • Dish's Materials

    • Wing eight

    • The amount of potato

    • The amount of carrot

    • The amount of onion

    • The amount of garlic

    • The amount of coconut milk

    • The amount of salt

    • The amount of chicken essence

    • Broccoli one

      • Butter 20g

  • Dish's Information

    • Difficulty: Simple

    • Time: One hour

    • Taste: Curry

    • Technology: Stew

First Step:

Blanch chicken wings with water.

Second Step:

Potatoes and carrots cut in small cubes, chopped garlic and Onions, because the wings are easy to cook and the potatoes are not soft.

Third Step:

The small fire stirs the onion and minced the garlic,pour in 10-15 grams of curry and stir-fry.

Forth Step:

Stir-fry the chicken wings and stir in the potatoes.

Fifth Step:

A bottle of coconut milk, 250ml, and 250ml water.

Sixth Step:

The fire was boiled, the small fire boiled to the curry, and the salt was put in (about 30 minutes). Juice, put some chicken essence.
