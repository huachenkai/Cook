Pasta

  • Dish's Materials

    • The right amount of spaghetti

    • The right amount of spaghetti sauce

    • The right amount of mozzarella cheese

  • Dish's Information

    • Difficulty: Simple

    • Time: Ten minutes

    • Taste: Salty delicacy

    • Technology: Roast

First Step:

Boil the water and put it in the pasta and cook it.

Second Step:

When you cook it, put it in a cold water and mix it together so that it doesn't stick together.

Third Step:

Drain and drain and load into a heated glass bowl.

Forth Step:

Then spread a layer of spaghetti sauce on top.

Fifth Step:

Sprinkle a layer of mozzarella on the surface,Heat the oven for 10 to 15 minutes.
