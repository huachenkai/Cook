Kung Pao Chichen

    • cucumber 75g

    • peanut

    • Butter 130g

    • cold water 75g

    • Apple grunt 450g

    • Sugar 50g

    • Cooking wine 20g

    • starch 10g

  • Dish's Information

    • Difficulty: Normal

    • Time: A few hours

    • Taste: Sour and Sweet

    • Technology: Baking
