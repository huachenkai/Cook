Pan-fried Goose Liver

  • Dish's Materials

    • goose liver four pieces

    • Slice of bread four pieces

    • Red wine 400g

    • Apple one

    • The right amount of Bitter chrysanthemum

    • The right amount of black pepper particles

    • The right amount of strawberry jam

    • The right amount of honey

    • The right amount of olive oil

    • The right amount of salt

    • Figs (dried) six caps

  • Dish's Information

    • Difficulty: Normal

    • Time: Fifteen minutes

    • Taste: Sour and Sweet

    • Technology: Fried

First Step:

Remove the skin from the skin, remove the core, remove the core, remove the dried FIG, and cook for 10 minutes in advance, and cover with the lid.

Second Step:

Prepare the sauce: add 1 tablespoon of olive oil to the pan, add a spoonful of strawberry jam, add 2 tablespoons of red wine, 1 tablespoon of honey, sprinkle with salt and pepper.

Third Step:

Keep the fire up until it thickens.

Forth Step:

The foie gras is salted with a dash of salt and pepper for 10 minutes, with a little flour on both sides.

Fifth Step:

Place a small amount of olive oil in the pan and fry the goose liver until it is hot.

Sixth Step:

Place prepared slices of bread, red wine apple slices, figs, chamomile, and a good strawberry sauce.
